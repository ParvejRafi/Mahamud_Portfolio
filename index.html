Documentary

PENSOL

Director  ·  Sound Design  ·  Storyboard  ·  Shootingboard Artist

This documentary explores the endangered heritage of the Lanoh, an Orang Asli group at risk of cultural extinction, through their unique musical artifact — the Pensol, a nose flute embodying their artistic and storytelling traditions. By highlighting the Lanoh's cultural significance and the Pensol's craftsmanship, the film raises awareness about preserving indigenous traditions and calls for action to safeguard their legacy.
